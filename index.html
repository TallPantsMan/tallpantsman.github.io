FEATURED CASE STUDY

Automating Processes During Parental Leave

Client

Mid-size digital agency (120 employees)

The Challenge

The Resource Manager went on maternity leave for 5 months. Their resourcing process lived in Google Sheets, manually updated every Friday. With 40+ projects and 20+ designers, manual updates were error-prone and time-consuming.

The Outcome

80+ total hours saved & 100% resourcing data accuracy

Our Approach

  • 01 Stepped in as interim PM support, learning their complex resourcing model in under a week.
  • 02 Designed and deployed a Google Apps Script to auto-pull data from project trackers, highlight overbooked staff with conditional formatting, and send weekly snapshots to team leads.
  • 03 Documented the entire custom automation architecture for the returning Resource Manager.

Key Outcomes

  • 100% accuracy in resourcing updates (zero missed allocations vs. ~5 errors per month previously).
  • Team leads received automated resourcing reports every Monday morning.
  • COO Win: "We expected to tread water; instead, our resourcing got better while [she] was out."
