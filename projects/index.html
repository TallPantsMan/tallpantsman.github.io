Resource Management During Parental Leave

Impact 80+ Hours Saved
Fidelity 100% Accuracy

Challenge: A mid-size digital agency's Resource Manager went on maternity leave. Their designer allocation and project planning lived in complex Google Sheets that required hours of error-prone manual updating every Friday.
